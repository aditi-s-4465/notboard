### Can’t decide what board game to play next? NotBoard keeps your games organized and recommends games you’ll actually love.

NotBoard centralizes your group’s full game collection in one place, helping you remember all of the games and track who owns each game. It also allows you to quickly narrow games by filtering by player count or category. Most importantly, NotBoard offers personalized recommendations based on the games your group already owns. By analyzing categories like strategy, thematic, or party games, the system identifies new games that your group is likely to enjoy.

Check out the deployed site here: https://notboard-cee1dc.gitlab.io/#/
